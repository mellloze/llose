    My First Week of College: Expectations vs. Reality
As a first-year BSIT student at EVSU – Carigara Campus, I walked into my first week of
college with a mix of excitement and nerves. Like many others, I had my own ideas about
what college would be like—but as the days went by, I quickly realized that expectations
and reality don't always match up.
Expectation: I’ll instantly make a lot of new friends.
Reality: I did meet new people—but it took time.
I thought I’d instantly click with everyone, but the truth is,
making friends in college is a, gradual process. Everyone’s adjusting, just like you. It was a
little awkward at first, but as the days went by, conversations started to flow naturally. Now, I’ve started to build real
connections with my classmates.
